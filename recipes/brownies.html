Brownies

Description

This easy brownie recipe takes minutes to whip up in one bowl and about 20 minutes to bake for a moist and fudgy crowd-pleasing treat!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1 1/4 flour
  • 1 cup butter
  • 4 eggs
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup walnuts

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x13-inch pan with baking spray.
  2. Whisk sugar, flour, melted butter, eggs, cocoa powder, vanilla, baking powder, and salt in a large bowl until combined.
  3. Spread the batter into the prepared pan. Decorate with walnuts.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until top is crinkled and a toothpick comes out with a few moist crumbs, about 20 to 30 minutes.
  5. Let cool completely in the pan on a wire rack before slicing into squares. Enjoy!
